# **App Name**: CourtIQ

## Core Features:

- Judicial Dashboard: Displays key performance indicators (KPIs) such as active cases, judgments, average duration, and success rate, along with national overview of pending case volumes for various courts.
- Case Outcome Predictor: Analyzes case type, court level, jurisdiction, and facts against provided evidence to predict case outcome, confidence score, and identify key strengths/risks.
- AI Case Law Research Engine: Facilitates deep legal analysis to extract legal issues and find precedents, offering tabs for Legal Issues, Similar Precedents, Argument Builder, Citation Notes, and Research Gaps.
- Automated Legal Drafting Studio: Generates structured drafts for legal documents (e.g. notices, contracts) based on party details, facts, and financial information, with an AI Legal Review tool highlighting risks and missing information.
- AI Court Stenographer: Transcribes court proceedings from audio file uploads or recordings.
- AI Judgment Summarizer: Condenses full-text court judgments into concise summaries.
- Structured JSON Output for AI Flows: All AI-driven features return structured JSON data to facilitate UI parsing and rendering, ensuring data integrity and compatibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary background: Midnight Navy (#0B1020) for a premium and trustworthy dark theme.
- Secondary background: Deeper Navy (#121A33) for card layers and content separation.
- Primary accent: Royal Blue (#5B8CFF) to indicate primary actions and emphasize authority.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern and readable look, with bold headings and tight tracking.
- Rounded corners (1.2rem radius) and soft shadows for a modern, approachable design.
- Use loading skeletons and spinners for asynchronous operations to provide visual feedback and enhance user experience.
